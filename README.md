# 💫 About Me

## Enthusiast Polymath | Founder | Future Stanford Applicant

I am a curious, self directed learner **rebuilding my life from scratch**
exploring and studying multiple fields simultaneously, refusing to be
confined to a single discipline or a single way of thinking.

Some call it ambitious. **I call it necessary.** 🔥

I realized that real education does not happen when someone hands you
a syllabus it happens when you decide, with full ownership and full
responsibility, to pursue knowledge because you genuinely cannot
imagine doing anything else.

---

## 🧠 How I Think About Learning

| | |
|---|---|
| ➗ **Mathematics** | teaches me how to think **precisely** |
| 🔬 **Science** | teaches me how to **question everything** |
| ✍️ **Writing** | teaches me that thought and expression are **the same thing** |
| 💻 **Code** | teaches me how to **build what I imagine** |
| 🚀 **Entrepreneurship** | teaches me that ideas mean **nothing without execution** |
| 📜 **History & Philosophy** | teaches me that every modern problem has **roots worth understanding** |

---

## ⚡ What I will Do and Doing

- 📐 Study **hard subjects** most people avoid
- 💻 Build **real projects** that solve real problems
- 🚀 Run a **startup** with real users
- ✍️ Write **publicly** about what I learn and struggle with
- 🤝 Lead a **small team** toward a shared goal
- 📖 **Document every step** of the journey

Not because someone told me to.
**Because I am genuinely unable to stop.** 🔥
